I lead design at the intersection of human behavior, product strategy, and craft.

I'm Peter, a Design Director at Match Group where I lead design across some of the world's most-used connection products. I came up through product design — years in the weeds of flows, prototypes, and research — before moving into leadership. That background shapes how I manage: I care deeply about craft, stay close to the work, and believe the best design leaders are still great designers.

Outside of work, I'm drawn to architecture, typography, and the kind of product writing that makes complex things feel obvious.

Product Design Branding Strategy Deck Design Workshop User Research Design Systems Design Operations Coaching Stakeholder Management

Philosophy

01
Research first. I make decisions grounded in user behavior and data — not assumptions or personal taste.
02
Systems thinking. Every feature I design considers how it fits into the larger product, brand, and design ecosystem.
03
Craft matters. I hold high standards for visual and interaction quality. The details aren't optional — they're the product.
04
Ship and learn. I favor tangible work in front of users over lengthy internal documents that go unread.
05
Build the team. Great design at scale comes from great designers. I invest in people as much as I invest in pixels.
06
Design is communication. Clear artifacts, frequent sharing, and direct feedback loops keep everyone aligned and moving.

Design Leadership

Great design leadership isn't about taste — it's about creating the conditions where talented people can do their best work. I focus on building psychological safety, clear craft standards, and decision-making processes that scale.

At Match Group, I've led multi-disciplinary design teams across product, research, and content design — partnering closely with engineering, product management, and data science to drive outcomes that matter.
